PiP Watcher
keep any page floating on top.

Paste a link, pop it into a picture‑in‑picture window, and keep working in the tab behind it. The floating window stays on top of every other app on your screen, not just your browser.

Note: many sites (e.g. most social platforms, banks) block embedding and won't render inside the floating window — you'll see a blank frame if that happens. Best results come from pages meant to be embedded, like video players or dashboards you control.
Uses your browser's native Picture‑in‑Picture window. Nothing is uploaded or stored.
