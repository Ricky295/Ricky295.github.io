📚 Rules of Cross-Weighted Math

Unlike standard Minesweeper where all directions weigh equal to 1, this variant assigns specific weights to adjacency:

  • Orthogonal neighbors (Up, Down, Left, Right) count as +2 points.
  • Diagonal neighbors count as +1 point.

An uncovered space displaying "4" can indicate exactly 2 orthogonal mines ($2 \times 2$), or 4 diagonal mines ($4 \times 1$), or a combination!

🎮 Double-Click Chording: Double-click any uncovered cell. If the total weight of surrounding flags matches the cell number, all other hidden squares around it will instantly dig open!
📱 Mobile Tap Controller: Toggle between the ⛏️ (Dig) and 🚩 (Flag) buttons on the panel to comfortably sweep with single-finger taps on phones!
