About Projector Sudoku

Projector Sudoku is designed for presentation screens and mental arithmetic training. The generated grid is guaranteed 100% solvable using exclusively the Box Hidden Single (Box HS) technique.

Gameplay Rules:

  • The puzzle is generated instantly based on your targeted logic difficulty.
  • Press Space or click Start to uncover the grid and start the micro-timer.
  • You cannot click, modify, or play any cells directly. It is a projector solver layout meant to be tackled mentally!
  • Once you solve the entire grid in your mind, press Space again.
  • The board replaces empty cells with the completed solution. You'll specify how many mistakes you made.
  • Your final score evaluates speed, logical steps, and precision using:
Scoring Algorithm: Score = ((1,000,000 / time_seconds) * steps) * fraction_done

Steps are the minimum Box HS moves required to solve the grid. Fraction done is calculated as (digits - mistakes) / digits, where digits is the total number of empty cells to fill.
