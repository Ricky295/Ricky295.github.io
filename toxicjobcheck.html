Scoring guide

  • 0–5: Mild issues – some rough edges, but nothing clearly toxic.
  • 6–12: Concerning – clear signs of unhealthy culture or leadership.
  • 13–20: Toxic – the environment is actively harming well‑being.
  • 21–25: Severely toxic – systemic issues causing burnout or long‑term stress.
